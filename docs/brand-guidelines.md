# Aerofly Brand Guidelines
## AirPulse Product Line

### Brand Voice

**Tone:** Inclusive, energetic, premium
**Personality:** Encouraging coach, not competitive drill sergeant

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "Every mile is yours" | "Beat your rivals" |
| "Find your pace" | "Be the fastest" |
| "Run your way" | "Crush the competition" |
| "Engineered for your journey" | "Dominate the road" |

**Prohibited terms:** fastest, beat, crush, dominate, destroy, kill it, beast mode

**CTA style:** Invitational, never aggressive
- Good: "Find Your Pace" / "Start Your Journey" / "Explore AirPulse"
- Bad: "Buy Now" / "Don't Miss Out" / "Act Fast"

### Social Proof
Always use specific numbers, never vague claims:
- Good: "Loved by 12,000+ runners in 40 countries"
- Bad: "The best running shoe on the market"

### Visual Identity

**Primary Colors:**
- Energetic Orange: #FF4500
- Deep Navy: #1A1A2E
- Cloud White: #F8F9FA

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, short (max 6 words)
- Body: Conversational, second-person ("you", "your")

**Logo Usage:**
- Minimum clear space: 2x logo height
- Never place on busy backgrounds
- Never stretch or recolor

**Photography Direction:**
- Real runners, real environments (not studio)
- Golden hour / blue hour lighting preferred
- Diverse body types and ages
- Show joy in movement, not strain

### Content Structure (Landing Pages)
1. Hero with emotional headline + product shot
2. Key features (max 3, benefit-led not spec-led)
3. Social proof section
4. Lifestyle imagery
5. Single clear CTA
