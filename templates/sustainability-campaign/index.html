The Circular Promise

From ocean plastic to running track — and back again.
🌊 Ocean-Bound Plastic — Every pair diverts 12 plastic bottles from waterways. Collected within 50km of coastlines by certified partners.
☀️ Solar Manufacturing — Our factories run on 100% renewable energy. Carbon-neutral certified since 2025.
♻️ Designed for Return — Mono-material construction means every shoe can be fully recycled into a new pair at end of life.
📦 Zero-Waste Packaging — Shoebox becomes storage. No plastic wrapping. Soy-based inks. FSC-certified cardboard.
